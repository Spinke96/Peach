Paisy Tech

The following contents of the Peach specific tech that you can practice and learn.
Float Canceling A Ariel
In short hold down on the joystick then press a jump button, if done correctly you should be floating at ground level. This is good for quick ground options since peach tilts are pretty lack luster. And is definetly a recommendation to learn a practice this when you first start to play these princesses.
JCFF/SHCFF/DJCFF
JCFF: Jump Cancel Fast Fall. SHCFF: Short Hop Cancel Fast Fall. DJCFF: Double Jump Cancel Fast Fall. In short JCFF/SHCFF/DJCFF are advanced movement options that allow less endlag on ariels or simply a mixup option to make you seem unpredicatble.
Turnip 0-Deaths
In short tunip combos aren't really needed to practice to get results, but they are deffinetly fun and challenging combos to perform.
